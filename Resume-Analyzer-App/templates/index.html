< Resume Analyzer >

Recruiters, especially those at big tech companies, often need to sift through thousands of resumes to identify suitable candidates. Consequently, developing tools to assist in this process is a natural progression. This app leverages NLP-related libraries in Python - such as Gensim, NLTK, spaCy, and RegEx - and data manipulation to create tools designed to support your hiring process.
